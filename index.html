üöÄ Installation Guide

  1. Download the RealTimeStancer_v1.0.zip file
  2. Extract the archive to get the content folder
  3. Copy the content folder to your Assetto Corsa installation directory
  4. Launch Assetto Corsa with CSP enabled
  5. Start a race or practice session (not showroom)
  6. Press Ctrl+Shift+S to open the app
  7. Adjust and enjoy your custom stance!
